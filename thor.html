Thor Odinson

Thor, the Prince of Asgard and God of Thunder, is a mighty warrior and protector of both his home realm and Earth. Known for his immense strength, bravery, and royal lineage, he carries himself with the confidence of a god but has grown into a leader who values humility and wisdom. While once driven by pride and the thrill of battle, he has learned the true weight of responsibility, fighting not just for glory but for those who cannot fight for themselves.

Abilities

As an Asgardian, Thor possesses superhuman strength, speed, endurance, and near-immortality. His command over lightning allows him to summon devastating storms and unleash powerful energy blasts. He wields Mjolnir (later Stormbreaker), granting him flight, weather control, and energy projection. He can summon lightning and eventually learns to harness his power without a weapon.

Backstory

Born to rule Asgard, Thor was once reckless and impulsive, but through trials and loss, he became a true protector. After first arriving on Earth, he formed an unlikely bond with its people, realizing that strength alone does not make a great leader. When cosmic threats emerged, he took up arms alongside the Avengers, defending Earth as if it were his own. Though he belongs to the stars, he has chosen to stand with his allies, proving that even a god can find a home among mortals.

First Comic Appearance: Journey into Mystery (1962) #83
