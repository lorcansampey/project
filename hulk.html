The Incredible Hulk

Bruce Banner, known as the Hulk, is a brilliant scientist with one of the most formidable alter egos in existence. While Banner is reserved, analytical, and constantly seeking control, the Hulk is pure, unrestrained power. The struggle between the two personas defines him, as he works to balance his intelligence with the destructive force within. Despite his immense strength, he strives to use his abilities responsibly, fighting alongside the Avengers to protect those who cannot protect themselves.

Abilities

The Hulk possesses nearly limitless strength, durability, and regenerative abilities, growing more powerful the angrier he becomes. His resilience allows him to withstand extreme physical and energy-based attacks, while his leaping ability enables him to cross vast distances in a single bound. In his Banner form, he is one of the world’s leading experts in gamma radiation, biology, and nuclear physics, providing invaluable scientific expertise to the Avengers. When Banner and Hulk are in sync, he becomes a strategic powerhouse, combining intellect with raw might.

Backstory

After an experiment with gamma radiation went wrong, Banner found himself transformed into the Hulk, a being of uncontrollable rage and devastation. Fearing the destruction he could cause, he spent years searching for a cure while avoiding those who sought to exploit his power. However, when global threats arose, he was given the chance to turn his curse into a force for good. As a member of the Avengers, he provides both unparalleled strength and scientific brilliance, proving that even the most uncontrollable power can serve a greater purpose.

First Comic Appearance: Incredible Hulk (1962) #1
